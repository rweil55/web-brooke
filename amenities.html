Brooke Pioneer - Amenities Overview



Rest rooms, water: in the town park on the south end of Wellsburg. Outhouse at mile 0.2 and 2.2

Bike shop, rentals: none

Restaurant, groceries: Kroger supermarket at the 27 St in Wellsburg. Several restaurants in Wellsburg, both along Charles Street and US2. Also marina restaurant at 12 th St and the river.

Camping, simple lodging: Blue Chip Motel on the south end of town

Swimming, fishing: Fishing in Ohio River. We have no information on water quality.

Wheelchair access: Good. Trail is paved and flat. The connection to the Yankee Trail in Wellsburg, has an up and down grade, where the trail drops about 20 feet from railroad grade to street level and back to railroad grade.

Summary None on Trail
